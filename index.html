About the studio

Small studio. Thoughtful work.

Nicole Design & Co. is a design and development studio based in Edmonton, Alberta. We help businesses create thoughtful digital experiences through product design, UX/UI, websites, and development.

Led by Nicole Buloran and supported by Nick Castillo, we combine design strategy and technical execution — without the overhead of a large agency.
